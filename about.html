OUR STORY

         One man dreamed of creating the most unbelievably delicious ice  
                                                     cream.


           It came out of the blue, but it made sense. He loved a scoop or 

          two of the good stuff and had often talked about making something

           with his own two hands; a real product for real people.


          He set to work in his small barn in Hampshire and when his wife

           decided to join him he named the company after her. On a cold

           winters day in December 2002 he walked across the field to the

          village pub and sold the very first tub of Unicone's ice cream.
DESIGNED FOR CHEFS

           We were soon working with top chefs around the country, designing 

                                    new flavours for their menus.



         Making our ice cream with the traditional batch method, we carefully 

           select the best ingredients; for example, single estate Colombian

              cacao, Madagascan bourbon vanilla and fresh milk and cream.



         We've tasted and tested literally thousands of recipes over the years

                            and won over 70 Great Taste Awards.
PLANET
             We are fortunate to live and work in an incredibly beautiful 

                            corner of our incredibly beautiful planet. 

            We make take-home tubs in plant based, dairy and lower calorie 

      flavours, as well as lower sugar treats for kids and a growing   range of

                                                  desserts.


                So cosy up and tuck into an old favourite, or go wild and

                                             try something new.


                   Our mission is to knock your socks off. Every time.
